Support Information for "Throw The Bad Virus" Game

Last updated: August 07, 2020

This page contains supporting information of our game.

The idea of this game is to throw the virus to the space by swiping up on the screen with your finger. The atmospheric region to which the virus will be thrown is determined by your swipe speed.
